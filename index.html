Jurassic Park (1993)

★★★★★
Release: June 11, 1993 Director: Steven Spielberg Producers: Kathleen Kennedy, Gerald Molen Main Actors: Sam Neill, Laura Dern, Jeff Goldblum Budget: $63M Revenue: $1.1B

Scientists visit a dinosaur theme park where genetically engineered creatures break loose and threaten human survival.

  • Groundbreaking CGI revolutionized Hollywood forever.
  • Based on Michael Crichton's novel.
  • Features the iconic T-Rex breakout scene.

The Lost World (1997)

★★★★☆
Release: May 23, 1997 Director: Steven Spielberg Producers: Colin Wilson, Gerald Molen Main Actors: Jeff Goldblum, Julianne Moore Budget: $73M Revenue: $618M

A rescue mission to Isla Sorna becomes chaos when dinosaurs are captured and brought to civilization.

  • Expanded dinosaur action and darker survival themes.
  • Famous San Diego T-Rex sequence.
  • Introduced Isla Sorna to the franchise.

Jurassic Park III (2001)

★★★☆☆
Release: July 18, 2001 Director: Joe Johnston Producers: Kathleen Kennedy, Larry Franco Main Actors: Sam Neill, Tea Leoni Budget: $93M Revenue: $368M

Dr. Alan Grant returns to dinosaur territory during a dangerous rescue operation.

  • Introduced the terrifying Spinosaurus.
  • Fast-paced survival-focused storytelling.
  • Expanded aerial dinosaur encounters.

Jurassic World (2015)

★★★★☆
Release: June 12, 2015 Director: Colin Trevorrow Producers: Frank Marshall, Patrick Crowley Main Actors: Chris Pratt, Bryce Dallas Howard Budget: $150M Revenue: $1.67B

A fully operational dinosaur theme park descends into disaster after a hybrid predator escapes.

  • Revived the franchise after 14 years.
  • Introduced Indominus Rex.
  • One of the highest-grossing films ever.

Fallen Kingdom (2018)

★★★☆☆
Release: June 22, 2018 Director: J. A. Bayona Producers: Frank Marshall, Belén Atienza Main Actors: Chris Pratt, Bryce Dallas Howard Budget: $170M Revenue: $1.3B

Dinosaurs face extinction once again as a volcanic eruption threatens Isla Nublar.

  • Darker gothic atmosphere and horror tone.
  • Introduced the Indoraptor hybrid.
  • Expanded the ethical questions of cloning.

Jurassic World Dominion (2022)

★★★☆☆
Release: June 10, 2022 Director: Colin Trevorrow Producers: Frank Marshall, Patrick Crowley Main Actors: Chris Pratt, Laura Dern, Sam Neill Budget: $265M Revenue: $1B

Dinosaurs now coexist with humans across the globe in a fragile ecosystem.

  • Legacy cast returns together for the first time.
  • Global dinosaur-human conflict storyline.
  • Epic franchise crossover finale.

Jurassic World Rebirth (2025)

★★★★☆
Release: 2025 Director: Gareth Edwards Producers: Frank Marshall Main Actors: Scarlett Johansson, Jonathan Bailey Budget: TBA Revenue: Upcoming

A new generation enters a dangerous prehistoric era where science and survival collide.

  • New reboot-style continuation.
  • Directed by Gareth Edwards.
  • Modern sci-fi cinematic tone.
